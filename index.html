Criteria for Acceptance:

At Density Ventures, we have a clear set of standards to ensure that our community remains as impactful as possible:

  • Willing to share expertise with fellow founders.
  • Promise to work in-person, in-office at least 4 days per week (unless you are traveling for sales)
