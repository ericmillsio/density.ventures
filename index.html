The Deal

  • Free dedicated desk space in shared room of founders @ 1871 (approx 6 months)
  • Co-mentorship and expertise from 10-20 elite founders in Density
  • YCombinator style small-group discussions
  • We take no equity or payment. This is very informal.

Acceptance Criteria

At Density, we have a clear set of standards to ensure that our community remains as impactful as possible:

  • Product built and on the market.
  • Actively selling to customers.
  • Scalable and venture backable business idea.
  • Plan to raise funding in 6-12 months.
  • Willing to share expertise with fellow founders.
  • Promise to work in-person, in-office at least 4 days per week (unless you are traveling for sales).
  • Currently have only founders, no full-time employees (flexible).
  • We're focused on startups that are building the future with AI technology and creating value with SaaS business models.

Why are we doing this?

Get great founders in a small room working independently on their startups. Most early founders are tired of WFH and coffeeshops.

Underlying motivation is to help 1871 regrow post-covid. The gameplan is get great founders in one room with a high likelihood of raising capital, and then go on to rent more 1871 space for their new team.
